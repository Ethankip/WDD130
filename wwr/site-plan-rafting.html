Overview

Purpose

Glacier Raft Company is the best river rental company in Montana. Here at glacier we seek to make river sports more accesible to the average person. We make it so that Anybody can easily rent all kinds of river gear for river sports. We carry tubes, rafts, lifejackets, even paddleboards. We are passionate here at Glacier in making river sports more affordable and fun!

Audience

Glacier Raft Company is for anyone who wants to experience the beauty and the thrill of river sports. From casual first goers to experienced rafting experts, we will ensure that you will find the perfect gear for your ability level. As long as you are ready for some adventure, we are happy to serve you! We have rented out to many college students, families, and many other river sports groups.
