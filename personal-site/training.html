Short Distance Runner

Are you working towards getting faster in the 400m, 800m or maybe even 300m hurdles? Well then this plan emphasizes low mileage and high intensity workouts as well as cross training to build strength. In no time at all you will be a speed demon with these workouts!

Middle Distance Runner

Are you trying to get faster in the Mile, 3200m or even 5k? Then we suggest a good mix of endurance and speed workouts with a heavy emphasis on stregnth. Building an efficient base is key at the start of this plan and once you combine that with fast closing speed, then you will dominate!

Long Distance Runner

This plan is made to help people build a great aerobic base to train for longer races like the 5k, 10k,and the half or full marathon. That means lots of mileage and tempo runs to help increase your lactate threshold and gain endurance. To avoid injuries recover is a big priority in this training plan.
